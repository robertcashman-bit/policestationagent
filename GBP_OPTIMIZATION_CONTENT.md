## BUSINESS DESCRIPTION (LONG FORM)

**Robert Cashman – Police Station Duty Solicitor (Kent)**

Robert Cashman is a qualified solicitor and accredited Police Station Duty Solicitor serving Kent since 2001. With over 35 years of criminal law experience and 21,000+ cases, he provides expert legal representation at all Kent custody suites.

**What is a Police Station Duty Solicitor?**

A Police Station Duty Solicitor is a qualified solicitor accredited by the Law Society to provide free legal advice at police stations under the Legal Aid scheme. Unlike unregulated "police station representatives" or agencies, a duty solicitor is a fully qualified solicitor with professional indemnity insurance, regulatory oversight, and the ability to represent clients from police station through to Crown Court.

**Services Provided**

Robert Cashman provides expert police station representation including:
- Legal advice before and during police interviews
- Representation at voluntary interviews under caution
- Bail applications and pre-charge representation
- Advice on rights in custody
- Higher Court Advocate representation in Crown Court

**Service Area**

Coverage extends across all Kent police stations including Medway (Gillingham), Maidstone, North Kent (Gravesend), Canterbury, Tonbridge, Folkestone, Ashford, Sittingbourne, Margate, Dover, Sevenoaks, and Tunbridge Wells.

**Availability**

Available 9am to late, including evenings, weekends, and bank holidays. Rapid response service aims to attend any Kent custody suite within 30-45 minutes.

**Qualifications & Experience**

- Qualified Solicitor (admitted since 2001)

- Higher Rights of Audience (Criminal) – Crown Court Advocate
- Practice Director experience at major Legal Aid practice
- 35+ years criminal law experience
- 21,000+ cases handled

**Why Choose a Qualified Duty Solicitor?**

When facing a police interview or arrest, choosing a qualified duty solicitor ensures:
- Professional indemnity insurance protection
- Regulatory oversight by the Solicitors Regulation Authority
- Full legal qualifications and ongoing training requirements
- Ability to represent from police station through to Crown Court
- Higher Rights of Audience for complex cases

**Legal Aid Coverage**

All police station representation is FREE under Legal Aid. This statutory right under PACE 1984 is not means-tested and applies to both arrested individuals and those attending voluntary interviews.
